HEAVEN ON EARTH


EST. DECEMBER 20, 2017

Located on “Chingrighata E.M Bypass” in the city of Kolkata, Heaven on Earth resturan and bars Kolkata is a Chingrighata institution. Adding some rock and roll to this famous upscale thoroughfare, HEAVEN ON EARTH Kolkata's menu features our local favorite, the Paneer Shaslikor, as well as a variety of fresh salads and signature cocktails. The connected Rock Shop offers rock ‘n’ roll-inspired apparel and collectible merchandise exclusive to Hard Rock Cafe Kolkata.

A TRULY UNIQUE DINING EXPERIENCE

The Cafe is located inside a 103-year-old heritage building called “Park Mansion.” Great care has been taken to renovate the house to preserve its unique historic elements while adding Hard Rock’s unique touch. This truly special dining venue is a multilevel, 5540 square-foot café that includes seating for 161 guests, a vibrant bar, and a live entertainment space. The unique, local music scene now has a new arena for performance that boasts a state-of-the-art audio-visual system and a food hub created for the people of Kolkata.

LOCAL FLAVOR YOU CAN'T MISS

Discover your favorites on the Hard Rock Kolkata menu, which features classic signature dishes like the Jumbo Combo, Classic Nachos, the Legendary Burger, and a host of premium beverages and cocktails. Acknowledging local tastes, the Kolkata menu has for the first time in India introduced a range of flatbreads and many local vegetarian favorites such as Paneer Shaslik, Quesadilla Burger, and more. Kolkata is a city where food is almost a religion, and food lovers will be thrilled by the menu that has been specially crafted with local tastes in mind. The tangy and spicy dishes are sure to delight palates across the city.
